Schedule

The Church's Service, Prayer, and Sunday School timings are listed below.

  • Evening Prayer - Every Saturday 7:00pm - 8:30pm
  • Morning Prayer - Every Sunday 8:30am - 9:30am
  • Holy Qurbana - Every Sunday directly following Morning Prayer from 9:30am to 11:30am
  • Sunday School - Every Sunday 12:00pm - 1:15pm

The service is conducted in Malayalam except for the fourth Sunday of every month where the service is conducted in English.
